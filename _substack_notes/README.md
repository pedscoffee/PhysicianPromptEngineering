# Substack Notes Library

A 90-day content library for Substack Notes during launch phase.

## Overview

This library contains **270 short-form posts** designed for Substack Notes:
- **90 Observations**: Insights, noticing, industry commentary
- **90 Calls-to-Action**: Direct invitations to visit website features
- **90 Personal Reflections**: Authentic, personal thoughts on physician work and AI

## Posting Strategy

**Daily Cadence**: 3 posts per day for 90 days

**Recommended Schedule**:
- **Morning** (7-9 AM): Observation post
- **Midday** (12-2 PM): Call-to-action post
- **Evening** (5-7 PM): Personal reflection post

## Content Philosophy

### Tone
- **Authentic**: Sounds like a real person, not a brand
- **Professional**: Maintains credibility and expertise
- **Conversational**: Approachable and relatable
- **Practical**: Focus on real-world applications

### Length
- Each note: 50-200 words
- Quick to read (30-60 seconds)
- Mobile-optimized for scrolling

### Goals
1. Build community and trust
2. Drive traffic to specific website features
3. Establish thought leadership
4. Demonstrate personal investment in the project
5. Create conversation opportunities

## Customization

Feel free to:
- **Reorder posts** based on current events or priorities
- **Modify content** to match your voice more closely
- **Add timely references** to recent developments
- **Skip posts** that don't feel authentic
- **Combine or split** posts as needed
- **Add hashtags** relevant to your audience

## Best Practices

### Engagement
- **Respond to comments** personally and quickly
- **Ask questions** to encourage dialogue
- **Share wins** from community members
- **Acknowledge feedback** publicly

### Timing
- **Test different posting times** to find optimal engagement
- **Avoid posting all three** within a short window
- **Space throughout the day** for maximum reach
- **Be consistent** with your schedule

### Content Mix
- **80/20 rule**: 80% value/insights, 20% promotion
- **Vary the topics** to keep feed interesting
- **Reference current events** when relevant
- **Share behind-the-scenes** development updates

### Analytics
Track which types of posts perform best:
- **Click-through rates** on CTAs
- **Comment engagement** on reflections
- **Likes/restacks** on observations
- **Follower growth** correlation with post types

## After Launch (Post-90 Days)

### Transition Strategy
- Reduce to 1-2 posts per day
- Focus on highest-performing content types
- Incorporate user-generated content
- Share community success stories
- Announce new features and updates

### Sustainable Cadence
- **3x per week**: Deeper engagement, less burnout
- **Mix of planned and spontaneous**: Keep it fresh
- **User spotlights**: Let community drive content
- **Feature updates**: Share new prompts and tools
