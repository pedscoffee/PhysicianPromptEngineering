Too specific: "Always use exactly 3 bullet points." Breaks when you need 4.
Too vague: "Format nicely." AI guesses randomly.

Best prompts balance specificity with flexibility. "Use 2-5 bullet points" or "Brief, scannable format."

Goldilocks principle applies to prompt engineering too.
