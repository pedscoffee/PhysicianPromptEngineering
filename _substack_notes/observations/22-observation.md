AI scribes love to write paragraphs. Long, meandering, overly detailed paragraphs.

This is why prompts matter. A good prompt says: "Be concise. Use bullets. Omit redundancy."

The difference between a raw AI scribe note and a well-prompted note is 10 minutes of editing versus 30 seconds of review.
