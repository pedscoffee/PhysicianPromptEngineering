"But doesn't AI hallucinate?"

Yes. And so do physicians when they're exhausted from hours of documentation.

The difference: you review AI output before signing. That review step catches errors whether they come from AI hallucinations or human fatigue.

Good prompts with clear examples dramatically reduce hallucinations. But review is always mandatory regardless.
