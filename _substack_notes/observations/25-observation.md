Burnout doesn't happen because of patient care. It happens because of administrative burden that crushes the joy out of medicine.

Reducing documentation time from 3 hours to 1 hour daily isn't a luxury. It's essential for sustainable practice.

You can't pour from an empty cup. And documentation is draining that cup every single day.
