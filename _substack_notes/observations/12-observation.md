Undercoding is expensive. Both for you and your organization.

AI can analyze your note and suggest appropriate CPT codes based on documented complexity. It catches the medical decision-making elements you might have undersold.

I've seen physicians discover they've been billing 99213s for encounters that clearly qualify as 99214s or higher. That adds up.
