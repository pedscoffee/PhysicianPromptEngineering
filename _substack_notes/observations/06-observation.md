The future of medical AI shouldn't be locked behind paywalls and proprietary algorithms.

Open source means you can see exactly how tools work. You can modify them for your specialty. You can share improvements with colleagues. No vendor lock-in. No subscriptions. No hidden agendas.

This is too important to leave to profit-driven companies alone.
