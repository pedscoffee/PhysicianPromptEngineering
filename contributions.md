# Share Your Clinical Prompts

## Why Contribute?

Every specialty faces unique documentation challenges. By sharing your refined prompts, you help colleagues save hours of trial and error while advancing the entire field of clinical AI documentation.

Your contribution could help thousands of physicians reduce burnout and improve patient care.

## How to Contribute

### Option 1: Quick Submission (Recommended)
Use our form below to submit your prompt directly. We'll review and add it to the library.

### Option 2: GitHub Contribution
1. Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/pedscoffee/PhysicianPromptEngineering/)
2. Join the [Discussions](https://github.com/pedscoffee/PhysicianPromptEngineering/discussions) to share ideas
3. Submit a pull request with your prompt file

## Submission Requirements

### Essential Information
- **Specialty/Setting** (e.g., Pediatrics - Outpatient, Emergency Medicine)
- **Prompt Title** (Clear, descriptive name)
- **Use Case** (What problem does this solve?)
- **Testing Notes** (How long have you used it? Approximate number of encounters?)
- **The Complete Prompt** (Copy-paste ready, under 5,000 characters)

### Quality Guidelines
- Include 3-5 real examples (de-identified)
- Test on minimum 10 patient encounters
- Verify compatibility with common EMR systems
- Remove any institution-specific information
