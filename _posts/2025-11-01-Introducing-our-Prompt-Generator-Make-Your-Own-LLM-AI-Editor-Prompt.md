## Customization Strategy

The power of prompt engineering lies in personalization. While our generator provides a strong foundation, optimal results come from iterating based on your specific needs:

### Few-Shot Examples: Your Secret Weapon

The most critical component of any clinical prompt is the few-shot examples section. These 3-5 sample outputs teach the AI your exact preferences:

- **Document structure** (problem-based vs system-based)
- **Language style** (formal vs conversational)
- **Brevity level** (detailed vs concise bullets)
- **Specialty terminology** specific to your practice

### Iteration Process

1. **Baseline Testing**: Use the generated prompt with 5-10 routine encounters
2. **Pattern Recognition**: Identify consistent issues or missing elements
3. **Example Refinement**: Update few-shot examples to address gaps
4. **Validation**: Test refined prompt on diverse encounter types
5. **Production Deployment**: Implement once achieving >90% acceptable output
