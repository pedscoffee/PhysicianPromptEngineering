# Introducing the Personal Prompt Snippet Manager

Your refined AI prompts deserve a home that respects both convenience and privacy.

-----

## The Prompt Management Problem

You've invested time crafting the perfect AI documentation prompt. It's been tested across dozens of encounters, refined through iterations, and finally produces exactly what you need. You paste it into a notes app, maybe save it in a document somewhere, perhaps email it to yourself.

Then three weeks later when you need it again, you can't find it. Or you find version 2 instead of version 4. Or you remember you had a great QI prompt for antibiotic stewardship but can't recall which file it's in.

Meanwhile, every available prompt management solution requires:
- Creating yet another account
- Uploading your prompts to someone else's server
- Trusting a third party with your workflow
- Paying a subscription fee for basic organization

There had to be a better way.

## Browser-Based Privacy

The [Personal Prompt Snippet Manager](https://physicianpromptengineering.com/prompt-manager/) solves this through a simple principle: your data never leaves your device.

Everything runs in your browser. No servers. No accounts. No uploads. Your snippets live in your browser's local storage—accessible only to you, on your device.

### What This Means for Healthcare

**HIPAA Compliance Made Simple**
- No data transmission to external servers
- No third-party access to your prompts
- No terms of service to review
- No business associate agreements needed

Your prompts are stored as a part of the cache on your device. No log-in is required, and it is easy to export and import between devices if you'd like to save the whole library or switch computers.

## The Privacy Architecture

Understanding how it works helps appreciate why it's secure:

1. **Local Storage API**: Your browser provides built-in storage that websites can access
2. **Client-Side Only**: All processing happens in your browser, nothing transmitted
3. **You Control Everything**: Export, backup, or delete your data anytime
4. **Open Source**: The code is transparent and auditable

Think of it like using a calculator on your phone—the tool runs locally, your data stays with you.

## Best Practices

### Tagging Strategy

Develop a consistent system from the start:
- **By specialty**: pediatrics, cardiology, surgery
- **By use case**: documentation, teaching, QI
- **By project**: research-2024, stewardship-initiative
- **By status**: production, testing, archive

### Version Management

Track prompt evolution systematically:
- Use semantic versioning (1.0, 1.1, 2.0)
- Note major changes in prompt text
- Keep working versions separate from stable
- Archive old versions instead of deleting

### Backup Workflow

Your data is only as safe as your backup strategy:
- Export monthly to local storage
- Save backups to secure cloud folder
- Keep separate work and personal collections

## Common Questions

**What happens if I clear my browser data?**
Your snippets are deleted. Regular exports prevent data loss.

**Can I sync across devices?**
Not automatically—that would require a server. Export from one device and import to another.

**Is there a character limit?**
Browser storage limits vary (typically 5-10MB), sufficient for thousands of prompts.

**Can I access offline?**
Yes, once loaded the page works completely offline.

**What about mobile?**
Works on mobile browsers, though editing is easier on desktop.
