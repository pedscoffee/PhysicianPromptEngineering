How to Copy

  1. Find the dot phrase you want to use
  2. Click anywhere on the phrase card
  3. You'll see a "Copied!" notification
  4. Paste directly into your EMR (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)

Note: Formatting is preserved, so your dot phrases will paste with proper line breaks and structure.
