From 10 Minutes to 90 Seconds: Dr. Martinez's Story

This week, I want to share a story that captures why this community exists.

EM

Dr. Elena Martinez

Pediatrician, Austin, TX

The Problem

AI scribes captured the conversation, but the output was verbose and generic. Parents needed specific, actionable guidance for their child's age. Elena needed it organized by topic (safety, nutrition, development, etc.) in clear, parent-friendly language.

She tried the generic prompts. Too formal. Not specific enough. Not her voice.

The Solution

Elena spent 30 minutes creating a custom prompt with examples from her actual after-visit summaries. She included:

  • 5 examples of her preferred format
  • Age-specific template sections (2-month, 6-month, 12-month, etc.)
  • Tone guidelines ("conversational but authoritative")
  • Organization preferences (bullets, not paragraphs)

First Test Result

90 seconds

From AI scribe output to finalized summary

What Happened Next

Elena shared her prompt on our contribution page. Within two weeks:

  • 50+ physicians downloaded it
  • 12 customized it for their specialties
  • 3 improved it with additional age ranges
  • The updated version went back into the library

This is community-driven development in action.
