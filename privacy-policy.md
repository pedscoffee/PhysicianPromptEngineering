      <h2 style="color: #2a7ae2; margin-bottom: 20px;">Introduction</h2>
      <p>Physician Prompt Engineering ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website physicianpromptengineering.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.</p>

      <h3 style="margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 10px;">1. Information We Collect</h3>

      <h4 style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 10px;">1.1 Information Collected Automatically</h4>
      <p>When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns through Google Analytics, including:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Browser type and version</li>
        <li>Operating system</li>
        <li>Pages you visit and time spent on each page</li>
        <li>Referring website addresses</li>
        <li>IP address (anonymized)</li>
        <li>General geographic location (country/city level)</li>
      </ul>

      <h4 style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 10px;">1.2 Information You Provide</h4>
      <p>We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to access most features of our website. However, if you choose to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Subscribe to our newsletter:</strong> We collect your email address through Substack (third-party service)</li>
        <li><strong>Contribute prompts via GitHub:</strong> Your GitHub username and any information you provide in contributions</li>
        <li><strong>Contact us:</strong> Any information you choose to provide in communications</li>
      </ul>

      <h4 style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 10px;">1.3 Local Storage</h4>
      <p>Some tools on our website (e.g., Prompt Manager, Course Progress Tracker, QI Project Tracker) store data locally in your browser using localStorage. This data:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Never leaves your device</li>
        <li>Is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties</li>
        <li>Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser data</li>
      </ul>

      <h3 style="margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 10px;">2. How We Use Your Information</h3>
      <p>We use the information we collect to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Understand how visitors use our website and improve user experience</li>
        <li>Analyze website traffic and usage patterns</li>
        <li>Identify technical issues and optimize website performance</li>
        <li>Send newsletters (only if you explicitly subscribe)</li>
        <li>Respond to your inquiries and requests</li>
      </ul>

      <h3 style="margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 10px;">3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies</h3>
      <p>We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. Specifically:</p>

      <h4 style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 10px;">Google Analytics</h4>
      <p>We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. Google Analytics uses cookies to track:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Number of visitors and sessions</li>
        <li>Pages viewed and user interactions</li>
        <li>Traffic sources and referrers</li>
        <li>Device and browser information</li>
      </ul>
      <p>You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the <a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on</a>.</p>

      <h4 style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 10px;">Managing Cookies</h4>
      <p>You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>View and delete cookies</li>
        <li>Block third-party cookies</li>
        <li>Block all cookies</li>
        <li>Delete all cookies when you close your browser</li>
      </ul>
