**Wound Care at Home**

**Keep the wound clean:**
1. Wash hands before touching the wound
2. Gently clean with mild soap and water
3. Pat dry with clean towel
4. Apply prescribed ointment if ordered

**Dressing changes:**
- Change dressing daily or if it becomes wet/dirty
- Use sterile or clean bandages
- Secure but don't wrap too tightly

**Signs of infection - call if you notice:**
- Increasing redness or warmth
- Red streaks extending from wound
- Pus or foul-smelling drainage

- Increasing pain or swelling
