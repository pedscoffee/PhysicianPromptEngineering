**Managing Fever at Home**

**Medications:**
- Acetaminophen (Tylenol) or Ibuprofen (Motrin/Advil) as directed
- *Do not give aspirin to children under 18*

**Comfort measures:**
- Light clothing and bedding
- Room temperature bath (not cold)
- Stay hydrated with clear fluids
- Rest as needed

**When to call us:**
- Fever >104°F (40°C)
- Fever lasting >3 days
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe headache or neck stiffness
- Confusion or lethargy
- Rash that doesn't blanch
- Persistent vomiting

**When to go to ER:**
- Difficulty breathing or blue lips
- Severe headache with vomiting
- Unresponsive or difficult to wake
- Seizure
- Signs of dehydration (no urine for 8+ hours, very dry mouth)
