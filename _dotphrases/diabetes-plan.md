**Target Ranges:**
- Fasting: 80-130 mg/dL
- 2 hours after meals: <180 mg/dL
- Bedtime: 100-140 mg/dL

## Hypoglycemia Management

**Symptoms to recognize:**
- Shakiness or trembling
- Sweating
- Rapid heartbeat
- Confusion or difficulty concentrating
- *Hunger*

**Treatment (Rule of 15):**
1. Check blood sugar
2. If <70 mg/dL, consume 15g fast-acting carbs:
   - 4 glucose tablets
   - ½ cup fruit juice
   - 1 tablespoon sugar or honey
3. Wait 15 minutes and recheck
4. Repeat if still <70 mg/dL
5. **Call 911** if unable to treat or loss of consciousness
