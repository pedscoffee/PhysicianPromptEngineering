Critical examination skills for identifying congenital anomalies and conditions requiring orthopedic intervention.

### Learning Objectives

- ✅ Identify abdominal findings and masses
- ✅ Perform hip dysplasia screening (Ortolani/Barlow maneuvers)
- ✅ Assess spine integrity and sacral dimples
- ✅ Recognize positional deformities vs structural abnormalities
