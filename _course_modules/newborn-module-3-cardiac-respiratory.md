## Module 3: Cardiovascular & Respiratory Assessment

Learn to differentiate innocent murmurs from pathological findings and recognize respiratory distress patterns.

### Learning Objectives

- ✅ Characterize heart murmurs and determine need for echocardiography
- ✅ Recognize signs of respiratory distress
- ✅ Differentiate central from peripheral cyanosis
- ✅ Develop differential diagnosis for cardiorespiratory findings
