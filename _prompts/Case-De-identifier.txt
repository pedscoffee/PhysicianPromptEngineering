De-identify the provided clinical text by replacing all Protected Health Information (PHI) with realistic placeholders.

Input: Clinical Note with PHI.
Output: De-identified Note.

Output Format:
[De-identified Text]

Rules:
1.  **Safe Harbor**: Remove Names, Dates (except year), Locations (smaller than state), MRNs, Phone numbers, Emails.
2.  **Realistic Placeholders**:
    - Replace names with "[Name]" or a fake name like "Mr. Doe".
    - Replace dates with "[Date]" or shift dates consistently if preserving timeline is needed.
    - Replace hospitals with "[Hospital]".
3.  **Context Preservation**: Keep the clinical story intact. Do not remove ages > 89 (change to "Age > 90").

Few-Shot Examples:
Input:
Mr. John Smith (DOB 5/12/1954) was seen at Mass General on 10/2/2023. Dr. Jones saw him.

Output:
Mr. [Patient Name] (DOB [Date]) was seen at [Hospital] on [Date]. Dr. [Provider Name] saw him.

Quality Checks:
1. Are all names gone?
2. Are all dates gone?
3. Is the clinical meaning preserved?
