TWEET 2/6:
Plain text format: .txt or .md files are universally compatible. Readable in any text editor, any device, any platform.

---

TWEET 3/6:
Avoid lock-in: If a tool only exports to proprietary format, you don't really own your prompts. Red flag.

---

TWEET 4/6:
Cloud storage: Keep master copies in Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive. Accessible from any job, any location.

---

TWEET 5/6:
Regular exports: Even if using specialized prompt tools, export to plain text monthly. Your backup, your control.

---

TWEET 6/6:
Future-proofing: You'll change jobs, tools will change, platforms will evolve. Portable prompts persist through change.
