TWEET 2/8:
Start with enthusiasts: Find the early adopters in your group. Their success stories convince skeptics better than any mandate.

---

TWEET 3/8:
Show, don't tell: Live demonstration beats PowerPoint. "Watch me document this visit with AI" is powerful.

---

TWEET 4/8:
Share prompts freely: Make your best prompts available to colleagues. Lower barrier to entry = higher adoption.

---

TWEET 5/8:
Measure and report: "Our group saved 200 collective hours last month" creates positive peer pressure.

---

TWEET 6/8:
Address concerns directly: Privacy fears, quality concerns, "cheating" stigma. Have honest conversations.

---

TWEET 7/8:
Provide training: Protected time for learning, not "figure it out on your own." Investment in training pays off.

---

TWEET 8/8:
Celebrate wins: Physician finishing notes same-day for first time? Acknowledge it. Positive reinforcement drives adoption.
