# Twitter Campaign for 90-Day Launch

Comprehensive Twitter strategy to build community, drive traffic, and establish thought leadership during launch phase.

## Overview

**Duration:** 90 days
**Daily Volume:** 2-3 tweets per day
**Total Content:** 200+ tweets
**Primary Goal:** Build engaged community of physicians interested in AI documentation

## Content Structure

### Daily Tweets (90 files)
Quick insights, tips, and observations
- **Format:** Single tweets (280 characters max)
- **Posting:** 1 per day, optimal times
- **Purpose:** Maintain consistent presence, provide quick value

### Tweet Threads (30 files)
Deeper dives on key topics
- **Format:** Multi-tweet threads (5-10 tweets each)
- **Posting:** 2-3 per week
- **Purpose:** Educational content, showcase expertise, drive website traffic

### Engagement Tweets (40 files)
Community building and conversation starters
- **Format:** Questions, polls, discussion prompts
- **Posting:** 3-4 per week
- **Purpose:** Build relationships, understand audience, increase visibility

### Success Stories (20 files)
User wins and testimonials
- **Format:** Story tweets with results
- **Posting:** 2-3 per week
- **Purpose:** Social proof, credibility, inspiration

### Behind-the-Scenes (20 files)
Development updates and personal journey
- **Format:** Authentic updates about building
- **Posting:** 2 per week
- **Purpose:** Transparency, connection, humanize the project

## Engagement Guidelines

### Reply Strategy
- **Response time:** Within 2-4 hours during business hours
- **Tone:** Helpful, professional, approachable
- **Always:** Thank people for sharing, ask follow-up questions
- **Link to resources:** When appropriate, not pushy

### Retweet Strategy
- **Amplify:** User success stories, community contributions
- **Share:** Relevant #MedTwitter discussions on burnout, documentation
- **Credit:** Always tag and thank original posters
- **Add value:** Quote tweet with your insight when relevant

### Community Building
- **Feature users:** Highlight community members using the tools
- **Ask questions:** Regular polls and discussion starters
- **Create hashtag:** Consider #PromptEngineeringWins for success stories

## Crisis Management

### If Negative Feedback
1. **Acknowledge quickly:** "Thanks for the feedback"
2. **Take offline if needed:** "Let's discuss via DM"
3. **Learn and adapt:** Incorporate valid criticism
4. **Don't delete:** Unless truly inappropriate
5. **Stay professional:** Never defensive or argumentative

### If Misinformation Spreads
1. **Clarify immediately:** Clear, factual correction
2. **Link to evidence:** Documentation, disclaimers
3. **Stay calm:** Don't amplify controversy
4. **Learn:** Prevent future misunderstandings

## Quick Start Guide

1. Review all daily tweets (ensure tone matches your voice)

## Best Practices

### Do's
✅ Be authentic and personal
✅ Reply to every genuine question
✅ Share both wins and struggles
✅ Give credit to community members
✅ Use threads for complex topics
✅ Ask questions to spark conversation
✅ Share specific, actionable tips
✅ Maintain consistent posting schedule

### Don'ts
❌ Over-promote (no more than 20% promotional content)
❌ Ignore negative feedback
❌ Use engagement bait ("RT if you agree...")
❌ Post only links without value
❌ Copy-paste same content from other platforms
❌ Use too many hashtags (max 4)
❌ Be defensive or argumentative
❌ Disappear for days without explanation
